FARZANA

Graphic Designer based in New York City

I was Born and raised in Bangladesh and moved to New York 2008. My work is influenced by the nature color and texture around me. After finishing High School I attended at Queens Borough Community College and graduated with a Digital Arts & Design. I decided to explore more about Graphic Design at Queens College.With an affinity for visual art, I try to ensure every design I create is rooted in compelling concepts, often telling a story. I occasionally do bridal henna; use my own design while i'm at it. When I'm not designing, you will find me being chatty with my loved ones; also making travel plans which is hardly ever executed due to lack of time.
